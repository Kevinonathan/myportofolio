Chicken Pilau

  • Ingredients:
    • 500g chicken (cut into pieces, bone-in or boneless)
    • 1 1/2 cups basmati rice (washed and soaked for 20-30 minutes)
    • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee (clarified butter)
    • 1 large onion (sliced thinly)
    • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
    • 2-3 green cardamom pods
    • 2-3 cloves
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 1 bay leaf
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder (optional, adjust to your spice preference)
    • Salt to taste
    • 4 cups water (or chicken broth for extra flavor)
    • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
    • Optional: 1/4 cup fried onions or raisins for garnish
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook the Chicken:
      • Heat 1 tablespoon of oil or ghee in a large pot over medium heat.
      • Add the chicken pieces and cook until they’re lightly browned on all sides. Remove and set them aside.
    2. Prepare the Pilau Base:
      • In the same pot, add the remaining tablespoon of oil or ghee.
      • Add the whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon stick, bay leaf, and cumin seeds) and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
      • Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown (this may take about 5-7 minutes).
      • Stir in the chopped tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
    3. Add the Spices:
      • Add the turmeric powder, ground coriander, garam masala, and red chili powder (if using). Stir well and cook the spices for 1-2 minutes until they become aromatic.
    4. Add the Chicken and Rice:
      • Return the browned chicken pieces to the pot and mix them with the spices.
      • Add the soaked and drained rice, and stir to coat the rice with the spice mixture.
    5. Cook the Pilau:
      • Pour in the 4 cups of water or chicken broth, and add salt to taste. Stir everything together.
      • Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it cook for 15-20 minutes (or until the rice is fully cooked and the water has been absorbed).
    6. Garnish and Serve:
      • Once the rice is cooked, gently fluff the pilau with a fork to separate the grains.
      • Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves and, if you like, top with fried onions or raisins for extra flavor and texture.
  • Tips:
    • You can add vegetables like peas, carrots, or potatoes for extra richness and flavor.
    • If you want a bit of extra heat, you can add green chilies along with the onions.
  • This Chicken Pilau pairs well with a side of yogurt or a simple salad. Enjoy the rich, aromatic flavors!

Chiken Tikaa Pizaa

Chicken Tikka Pizza is a delicious fusion of Indian and Italian flavors! Here's a simple recipe to make it:

Ingredients:

  • For the Chicken Tikka:
    • 500g boneless chicken (cut into small cubes)
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tablespoon oil
  • For the Pizza:
    • 1 pizza base (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or use a little bit of tomato ketchup)
    • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 1 small red onion (thinly sliced)
    • 1/2 bell pepper (thinly sliced)
    • Fresh cilantro/coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Instructions:
    1. Marinate the Chicken:
      • In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, salt, and oil.
      • Add the chicken cubes to the marinade and mix well to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour (overnight for best results).
    2. Cook the Chicken Tikka:
      • Heat some oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the marinated chicken for about 5-7 minutes, or until fully cooked. Set it aside.
    3. Prepare the Pizza Base:
      • Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
      • If you're using a store-bought pizza base, spread a thin layer of pizza sauce (or ketchup) on it.
    4. Assemble the Pizza:
      • Evenly spread the cooked chicken tikka over the pizza base.
      • Add thin slices of red onion and bell pepper.
      • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
    5. Bake the Pizza:
      • Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges of the crust are golden.
    6. Garnish and Serve:
      • Once out of the oven, sprinkle some fresh cilantro leaves on top for extra flavor.
      • Slice and enjoy your Chicken Tikka Pizza!
